> CR90 Corvettes, also known as Corellian corvettes or blockade runners, were consular vessels manufactured by the Corellian Engineering Corporation. While the CR90 would see initial use within the late Galactic Republic and Imperial Senate, many vessels would be appropriated by the early rebellion and Rebel Alliance against the First Galactic Empire, despite not initially being designed as a combat-oriented vessel. This block kit contains a small amount of blocks to enable a more accurate build of such a corvette and go well with my blueprint of the [CR90 Corellian Corvette](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3686217395).

[![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/enenra/gfa/master/assets/mod_banner.png)](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2978742816)

![img](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/enenra/gfa/master/assets/header_features.png)
The mod contains three blocks:

* CR90 Bridge Window
* CR90 Variable Array Sensor Unit (Antenna)
* CR90 Ter58 Ion Turbine Drive (Thruster)

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/enenra/gfa/master/assets/header_notes.png)

* The turrets seen in the screenshots were made for the CR90 as well, but have been released in the [GFA - Rebel Turrets](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3452472766) mod.
* I have no current plans to make more blocks for this block kit.
